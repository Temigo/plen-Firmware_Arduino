Detailed Description

Management namespace of pin mapping.

Please give the standard Arduino libraries the methods returning values. The methods are evaluated at compile time, so there is no overhead at runtime.

Note
It helps your understanding that to refer the PLEN2's circuit and schematic. -> https://github.com/plenproject/plen__baseboard
See also
Arduino Micro's pin mapping -> http://arduino.cc/en/Hacking/PinMapping32u4
